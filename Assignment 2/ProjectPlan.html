Customer Mission

We are a small family-run local business, operating in Kirwan since 2012, hoping to improve our
business by getting on the Internet.

Goal

So the bakery can attract new customers through their website. Since these days most people are probably always
surfing the internet which could be a huge advantage to the bakery if they have a website up. Through this butterfly effect,
the bakery has a high chance of increasing their sales

Success evaluation

This goal will be proven successful when the bakery would see an increase on new customers through their newsletter
and with a significant increase in customers, the bakery should also expect an increase in product sales after the website has been put up

Target audience

Content analysis

  • Homepage: Displays the menu the bakery provides and has a welcome text and show customers reviews.
  • About Us: Will briefly explain the history of the bakery
  • Product Page: Will show a more detailed picture of the product and give a brief explanation of the product
  • Weekly Special Page: Shows which items are currently on sale
  • Sign Up page: A page where users can fill their details and get weekly newsletter about the items they are interested in

Page design

Home page: Welcome text will show some customer reviews to the bakery and Menu will show products provided by the bakery


Product/Item page: Item description will show a detailed picture of the product and a brief description of the product


About Us page: About Us will explain the brief history of the bakery and explains what it is


Weekly specials page: Shows a number of products that are currently on sale and also a button that leads to the sign up page


Sign Up page: A form where users can input their details and get weekly newsletter on the items they are interested in
